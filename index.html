April 25, 2017

Tech Maven.
Musician.
Makeup Enthusiast.

After years in academia & non-profit admin, creating through code is my pefect fit. A non-traditionalist in many ways, I got a self-taught web dev education and moved on to a bootcamp for some extra skills. Learning to code, was the best decision I've made in my life thus far. Post Bootcamp, I am honing my skills by learning every day. As a developer, I will need to keep learning new frameworks and languages, be flexible, and connect with various teammates throughout my career, and I couldn't be more excited for what the future holds.

Connecting my past to my present, my passion has always been people. When I code, I'm solving problems to benefit people's lives. Whether it's fixing bugs, implementing a better design, or adding brand new features, I code knowing that whatever I contribute will have an impact on living, breathing people.

When I'm not coding, I like to write and record music, stay up to date on the latest makeup trends, watch horror movies with basset hounds, and think about how fantastic Idris Elba would be as the next Doctor Who.
